Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

This policy explains what personal data MULTIPLY VENTURES LTD collects, why we collect it, and your rights under UK GDPR.

1. Who we are

GenView is a brand currently operated by MULTIPLY VENTURES LTD, which acts as the data controller for personal data collected through this website. GenView will be incorporated as its own entity in due course, at which point this policy will be updated accordingly.

If you have any questions about this policy or how we handle your data, you can contact us at:
Email: hello@genview.uk

2. What data we collect

We collect only the following personal data:

  • Email address — provided voluntarily when you submit the sign-up form on our website.

We do not collect names, phone numbers, payment information, or any other personal data through this website.

3. Why we collect it and our lawful basis

We collect your email address to add you to our pre-launch mailing list so we can notify you when GenView launches and share relevant updates.

Our lawful basis for processing your email address is consent (UK GDPR Article 6(1)(a)). By submitting the form, you are giving us permission to contact you for this purpose.

4. How we store your data

Email addresses submitted through our website are stored securely in Google Sheets, via Google Apps Script. Google LLC acts as a data processor on our behalf and processes data in accordance with Google's privacy policies and the EU–US Data Privacy Framework.

We retain your email address until you request deletion or unsubscribe, at which point we will remove it promptly.

5. Who we share your data with

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data is shared only with:

  • Google LLC — as our data storage processor (Google Sheets / Google Apps Script).

We will never share your data with any other third party without your explicit consent, unless required to do so by law.

6. Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure — ask us to delete your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Restriction — ask us to restrict how we use your data.
  • Objection — object to our processing of your data.
  • Withdraw consent — withdraw your consent at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of processing carried out before withdrawal.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@genview.uk. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Cookies

This website does not use cookies, tracking pixels, or any analytics tools. No data is collected about how you browse or interact with this site beyond what you submit directly through the sign-up form.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to check back periodically.

9. Complaints

If you are unhappy with how we handle your data, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the UK's data protection authority:

Information Commissioner's Office (ICO)
ico.org.uk/make-a-complaint
Telephone: 0303 123 1113
